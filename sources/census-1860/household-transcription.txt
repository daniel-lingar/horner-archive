NOTES
- Dollar columns apply to head of household row only (standard 1860 Schedule 1 layout).
- Spencer Horner (line 5) is the son; John T. Homer is the patriarch (b. ~1808; age rounds to 52 in 1860).
- Combined documented valuation on adjacent lines: $1,100 real estate + $1,000 personal property (Spencer household).
- William Riley's separate household shows higher real-estate valuation ($900) than his father's ($200), consistent with two propertied farms in the same township cluster.
- Patents on file (1848 cash entry + 1857 Patent 19B) document additional land not fully reflected in these census columns — see OQ-17/OQ-19.
