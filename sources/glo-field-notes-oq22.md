## Findings for Sections 19 & 22

1. **Terrain split:** Upland lines consistently "hilly & Rocky soil poor timber Pine & Oak"; Section 19–20 north line notes bottom land as **"1st rate soil"** with Oak/Gum timber.
2. **Mulberry corridor:** Section 21–22 north line (29 Dec 1845) — **"Mulberry 110 links wide runs S W"**; Section 19–20 line (5 Jan 1844) — **"West Mulberry … 144 links wide runs West."** Aligns with Little Mulberry Creek geography in family narrative.
3. **Water / roads:** Multiple brooks (15–40 links wide); roads crossing section lines — useful for reconstructing 1864 approach routes.
4. **Pre-patent occupancy:** Surveyor entries name **John Arrington's field**, **G. Homers field**, and **Subanks field** on Section 19–22 lines (1844–1845). Treat as occupation evidence, not title; Spencer Horner's patent chain begins 1857.
5. **Corners:** Standard 1844–1845 GLO quarter-section and section corners established with witness trees (Oak, Pine, Gum, Hickory).
