Subject: Fiscal Sponsorship / Partnership Request — Horner Civil War Archive [Date] [Recipient Name] [Organization] [Address or Email] Dear [Recipient Name], My name is Daniel Lingar, and I am writing from Clarksville, Arkansas, to ask whether [Organization] would be willing to discuss a possible fiscal sponsorship or partnership for the Horner Civil War Archive. The Horner Civil War Archive is a descendant-led public history project focused on preserving, researching, and interpreting the Civil War-era violence and community history surrounding the Horner, Hill, and Acord families of Johnson County, Arkansas. The project centers on the August 1864 killing of John Spencer Horner in Floyd Township and uses that event as a focal point for broader public education about guerrilla violence, divided communities, land records, military service, memory, and rural Arkansas history. The project already has a public website and GitHub archive: • Website: https://daniel-lingar.github.io/horner-archive/ • GitHub: https://github.com/daniel-lingar/horner-archive At this stage, I am not asking for a large commitment. I am seeking a first conversation about whether [Organization] might be able to support the project through one or more of the following: • Fiscal sponsorship for grants and donations • Letter of support • Advisory or archival guidance • Public program partnership • Meeting space or event support • Student intern or faculty partnership • Help connecting with local history, humanities, or preservation funders This project is still lean, but it is not starting from zero. The research foundation is already built. What it needs now is a credible community or institutional partner so the archive can move from personal research into a durable public history initiative. The first 12-month goal is practical: formalize sponsorship or advisory support, improve the public-facing archive, build educational materials, host at least one public presentation, and submit early grant applications. I believe this project aligns with [Organization] because [insert tailored paragraph]. I would be grateful for a short meeting or email conversation to ask whether this project may fit your mission or whether you can recommend a more appropriate sponsor or partner.
