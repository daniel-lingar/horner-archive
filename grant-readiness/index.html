Why Fiscal Sponsorship First?

The project already has a public website, GitHub archive, source-based research, mapping files, grant material, and direct descendant leadership. Fiscal sponsorship is the fastest practical path to grants, donations, letters of support, advisory review, and local legitimacy without immediately forming an independent nonprofit.

First Target Partners

  • Johnson County Historical Society
  • University of the Ozarks
  • Arkansas State Archives
  • Johnson County / Clarksville public library partners
  • Arkansas Humanities Council or regional humanities partners
