### Letter body (copy/paste)

```
Monica King, Circuit Clerk
Johnson County Circuit Court
P.O. Box 189
Clarksville, AR 72830

Dear Clerk King,

I am an independent researcher and Johnson County resident documenting Civil War-era history in Floyd and Mulberry Townships. I respectfully request assistance locating probate/estate files for two 1865 administrations indexed in your court records.

1. Estate of Spencer Horner, 1865
   Administrator: Dr. John Turner Horner (Johnson County, Arkansas)

2. Estate of William R. Horner, 1865
   Administrator: Mary Ann Horner (widow)

Prior research notes indicate these estate papers were listed in the courthouse index under "pigeon hole 6." A physical search in October 1973 did not locate the files. I am hoping they may have been re-filed, microfilmed, or transferred since that date.

Specifically, I am seeking:
- Estate inventories (real and personal property)
- Administrator's bonds and court orders
- Creditor claims and any court-ordered sales of land
- Final settlements and heir distributions
- Any administrator's deeds conveying the Spencer Horner homestead (160 acres, Patent/Certificate 19B, Township 12 North, Range 24 West, Sections 19 and 22, Floyd/Mulberry Township area)

If the original files are not in your current custody, please advise:
- Whether Johnson County probate records from 1865 were microfilmed (and the film or volume citation)
- Whether the files were transferred to the Arkansas State Archives or another repository
- The correct index entry, box/folder number, or volume and page if the "pigeon hole 6" designation has been superseded

I am also interested in any related deed recordings (1865–1870) where Permelia Turner Horner, Mary Ann Horner, or John Turner Horner (as administrator) conveyed the above-described land.

I will pay applicable copy fees. Digital scans or photocopies are preferred; in-person examination in Clarksville is also possible.

Thank you for your assistance.

Sincerely,
Daniel Bret Lingar
Clarksville, AR 72830
lingardaniel1@gmail.com
```
